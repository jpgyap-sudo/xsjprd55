🔄 TLL Pipeline Flow

The TLL runs in 8 sequential steps. Each step feeds into the next. Below is the explanation of the latest cycle.

🔍 Pattern Discovery — Why These Patterns?

Patterns are discovered by analyzing feature buckets (RSI, timeframe, side, volatility, etc.) across resolved signals. Each pattern shows the conditions where a strategy performs well or poorly.

Loading pattern explanations...

🎯 Skill Generation — From Patterns to Skills

Skills are generated from high-confidence patterns (win rate >= 0.6). Each skill is a reusable trading rule that can be applied to future signals.

Loading skill explanations...

⚖️ Weight Tuning — How Strategies Are Scored

Strategy weights are calculated using a 3-factor blend: 50% regime affinity, 30% risk-adjusted score (Sharpe, Sortino, max drawdown), 20% historical performance.

Loading weight explanations...

🔒 Strategy Healing — Why Strategies Are Quarantined

Strategies with win rate < 25% are automatically quarantined. The healing system also uses Ollama to generate richer suggestions for improvement.

Loading healing explanations...

🏆 Strategy Tournament — Head-to-Head Rankings

Every TLL cycle runs a round-robin tournament comparing all strategies head-to-head. Rankings use Elo scoring with 40% win rate, 30% avg PnL, 30% risk-adjusted score.

Loading tournament explanations...

🌊 Market Regime Detection — Current Market State

The regime detector classifies the market into 5 states (trending, choppy, ranging, quiet, mixed) based on volatility and recent win rates. This directly affects strategy weights.
